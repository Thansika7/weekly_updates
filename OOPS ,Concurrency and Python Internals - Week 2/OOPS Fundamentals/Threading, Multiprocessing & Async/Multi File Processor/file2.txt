Information Technology:

(IT) involves the use of computers, storage, networking, and physical devices to create, process, store, secure, and exchange all forms of electronic data. It is a broad field crucial for modern business, covering software development, system administration, and technical support. 
Key aspects of IT include:

    Infrastructure & Hardware: Managing servers, computers, and data centers.
    Software & Applications: Developing and maintaining software solutions.
    Networking: Building and maintaining communication systems.
    Security: Safeguarding data and systems from threats.
    ITeS (IT-enabled Services): Enhancing business quality through IT-driven services. 

IT professionals are responsible for troubleshooting, maintaining, and developing technology to support operational efficiency.
